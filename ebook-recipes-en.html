10 Easy Sweet Potato Recipes

A simple recipe ebook for everyday home cooking

Fresh sweet potatoes are easy to cook, filling, and suitable for many styles of meals and snacks. This ebook gives you 10 simple recipe ideas you can make at home with practical ingredients.

1. Steamed Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 2–4 sweet potatoes
  • Water for steaming

Steps

  1. Wash the sweet potatoes well.
  2. Place them in a steamer.
  3. Steam for 25–40 minutes depending on size.
  4. Check with a fork until soft.
  5. Serve hot.

Serving idea: Great for breakfast or tea time.

2. Roasted Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 3 sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Wash and dry the sweet potatoes.
  3. Place on a baking tray.
  4. Roast for 40–50 minutes until soft.
  5. Slice open and serve warm.

Serving idea: Enjoy on its own or with butter.

3. Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika or black pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and cut into fries.
  2. Toss with oil and seasoning.
  3. Spread on a tray.
  4. Bake at 200°C for 25–30 minutes.
  5. Turn once halfway.

Serving idea: Serve with chili sauce or mayonnaise.

4. Mashed Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 3 sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Steam or boil the sweet potatoes until soft.
  2. Peel and mash in a bowl.
  3. Add butter, milk, and salt.
  4. Mix until smooth.
  5. Serve warm.

Serving idea: Good as a side dish with chicken or fish.

5. Sweet Potato Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 sweet potato, diced
  • 6 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the rice.
  2. Put rice, sweet potato, and water in a pot.
  3. Simmer until the rice becomes soft.
  4. Stir from time to time.
  5. Add salt and serve.

Serving idea: Comforting meal for breakfast or light dinner.

6. Sweet Potato Dessert Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 4 cups water
  • Rock sugar or sugar to taste
  • 2 slices ginger
  • Pandan leaf (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water with ginger.
  2. Add sweet potato cubes.
  3. Simmer until tender.
  4. Add sugar and pandan if using.
  5. Serve hot.

Serving idea: A simple dessert for the family.

7. Sweet Potato Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mashed sweet potato
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Oil for pan frying

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a batter.
  2. Heat a pan with a little oil.
  3. Spoon batter into the pan.
  4. Cook both sides until golden.
  5. Serve warm.

Serving idea: Nice with honey or condensed milk.

8. Crispy Sweet Potato Balls

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed sweet potato
  • 1 cup tapioca flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix mashed sweet potato with tapioca flour and sugar.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Heat oil over medium heat.
  4. Fry until golden.
  5. Drain and serve.

Serving idea: Perfect for snacks or small business selling.

9. Honey Butter Baked Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 2 roasted sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Roast the sweet potatoes until soft.
  2. Slice them open.
  3. Add butter and honey.
  4. Let it melt into the sweet potato.
  5. Serve immediately.

Serving idea: Simple, sweet, and suitable for dessert.

10. Sweet Potato Meal Prep Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 roasted sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup vegetables
  • 1 protein of choice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Roast or steam the sweet potatoes.
  2. Prepare vegetables and protein.
  3. Arrange in a bowl.
  4. Season lightly.
  5. Serve as a balanced meal.

Serving idea: Good for lunchbox preparation.

Storage Tips

  • Keep sweet potatoes in a cool, dry place.
  • Do not store in the fridge unless cooked.
  • Use within 1–2 weeks for best quality.

Simple Cooking Tips

  • Smaller sweet potatoes cook faster.
  • Steaming keeps the inside moist.
  • Roasting gives a sweeter taste.
  • Sweet potatoes work for savoury and sweet recipes.
