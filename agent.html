How rewards work

Independent rewards may be given for valid, verified results

Reward amounts are not automatic. They depend on the quality of the lead, whether the buyer is genuinely new, whether the order is confirmed, and whether the case passes verification.

Subject to verificationEvery referral or buyer introduction will be checked before reward approval.
Subject to confirmationRewards are generally based on real completed orders or approved outcomes, not just contact sharing.
Independent arrangementReward handling can be discussed separately depending on the buyer type, order size, and contribution.
